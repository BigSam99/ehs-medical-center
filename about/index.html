About

The Premier Choice in Healthcare Staffing

One day, some healthcare professionals got to talking to some staffing professionals. And here’s some of what they said:

Administrators - Wouldn’t it be great if a staffing agency could help us strategically, not just provide staff…”

Caregivers - Wouldn’t it be great if a staffing agency could see us as part of its heart and soul, not just as billable hours…”

Staffers - Wouldn’t it be great if we could build a staffing agency infused with quality care and a deeper understanding of client needs…”

Amidon Nurse Staffing was created to be all those things and more. Amidon Nurse Staffing is staffing that cares for everyone in the Healthcare Staffing Cycle.
